Project Proposal: Crested Geckos

Mission Statement

To create a comprehensive website that provides information and resources about geckos.

Goals the the site will achieve

  1. Provide accurate and up-to-date information about geckos.
  2. Help people learn about the different types of geckos.
  3. Encourage people to appreciate and protect geckos.
  4. Information on gecko care, including diet, housing, and breeding

Model Site

I would like to model the layout of my site after the website. I chose this site because CBREPTILE.COM is a well-organized and easy-to-navigate website that provides a wealth of information about geckos The site has a comprehensive guide to the different types of geckos, as well as information on gecko care, housing, and breeding. The site also has a forum where users can share information and ask questions.

Content

  • Homepage

    This page will provide an overview of the site and its contents.

  • Types of Geckos

    This page will describe the different types of geckos, including their appearance, habitat, and behavior.

  • Gecko Care

    This page will provide information on how to care for geckos as pets.

Target Audience

The target audience for my site is people who are interested in geckos. This includes people who want to learn more about geckos, people who are thinking about getting a gecko as a pet, and people who are interested in conservation efforts for geckos.

Presentation Requirements

My proposal must be presented within a fixed-layout. The content of the document should be centered within the web page and margins must be zeroed out within the browser.
